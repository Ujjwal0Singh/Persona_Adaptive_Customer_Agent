BILLING POLICY AND REFUNDS

Subscription Billing Cycle
---------------------------
All subscriptions are billed monthly on the calendar day you originally
signed up. If you signed up on the 31st of a month, billing will occur on
the last day of months with fewer days (e.g., the 28th in February).

Refund Eligibility
-------------------
- Refunds are available within 14 days of the original charge for annual
  plans, and within 7 days for monthly plans.
- Refunds are NOT available for usage-based overage charges once the usage
  has been recorded on our servers.
- To request a refund, contact billing-support@example.com with your
  account email and invoice number.

Failed Payments
----------------
If a payment fails, we retry the charge automatically on days 1, 3, and 7
after the failed attempt. After three failed retries, the account is
downgraded to the Free tier. No data is deleted during a downgrade.

Updating Payment Method
-------------------------
1. Go to Account Settings > Billing.
2. Click "Update Payment Method."
3. Enter the new card details and click "Save."
4. The new card will be used starting from the next billing cycle.

Enterprise Invoicing
----------------------
Enterprise customers on annual contracts may request invoice-based billing
(NET-30 terms) by contacting their account manager. Invoice-based accounts
are not subject to automatic card retries.
